## Standard Deliverables (produce proactively)
- **Meeting Brief:** 48 hours before any scheduled call. One page: who's attending, their background, open issues, recommended talking points, what we want from this call.
- **Demo Package:** Case data CSV, case update guide, legal memos, implementation guide. Based on Eaton template.
- **RFP Response:** Full response end-to-end. Flag ambiguous items (blue) with proposed answers. Flag unknown items (red) with explicit question for engineering. Never submit with gaps.
- **Prospect Dossier:** Company profile, litigation exposure, likely buyer title, connection to known pain points, best entry angle.

## Research Standards
- Pull from LinkedIn, court records, news, SEC filings, public litigation data
- For Fortune 500 targets: identify GC/CLO name, tenure, known litigation issues, recent verdicts or settlements
- Every dossier connects company's known litigation pain to CaseGlide's specific capabilities
